Requirements for the Preparation of a Research Article

In process of preparing a research article or the text of a research paper, use the Times New Roman font, size 12, A4 paper size (210x297 mm) with margins: top - 25 mm; left - 30 mm; bottom - 25 mm; right - 15 mm. The editor should be as MS Word 2007 (or later versions). The title of the research article or paper is printed in capital letters (line spacing 1). Then, 2 lines after the title, the initials and surnames of the authors, the name of the organization (including the city and country) that they represent, and the email addresses of the authors are printed. After one line, abstracts in Kyrgyz, Russian and English languages are required. After that, after 2 lines, the text of the research article or paper is printed, the line spacing is 1,5. Illustrations (figures), tables and a list of references (line spacing 1) should be included in the text of the research article or paper. The volume of the text of the research article or paper should not exceed 5 pages.

The authors bear full responsibility for the content, style and quality of prepared research article or paper.
