Registration and Publishing Fees

Participant registration fee for local people – 3000 KGS for online and offline participation and publication of an article (for online participation: including VAT and transportation costs Bishkek - Issyk-Kul - Bishkek);

Participant registration fee for foreign people – 10 000 KGS or in US $ at the exchange rate on the day of payment for online and offline participation (for online participation: including VAT and transportation fees Bishkek - Issyk-Kyl - Bishkek)

Abstracts and texts of scientific papers will be accepted and published in the Journal only subject to payment of the registration fee for participation in the conference;

Cost for publishing of articles(without participation) is 2 000 KGS or in US $ at the exchange rate on the day of payment..

To become a Conference Participant, you need::

1. Review periodically the web-site of the III IAEEE International Scientific and Practical Conference (icee.kg);

2. Fill out the online registration form on the Conference web-site (icee.kg);

3. Be sure to specify abstracts (brief description of the research paper / article) in the online registration form;

4. Send a research paper / article, presentation to IAEEE email: seismokg@mail.ru;

5. Comply with the deadlines for sending, technical parameters and requirements for preparing of research papers / articles (sample indicated on the conference web-site);

6. For accommodation in Bishkek, book a room at the “Jannat Regency” Hotel or “Golden Dragon” Hotel according to the dates of arrival and departure. Details, contacts, prices (price lists of hotels) are specified on the Conference web-site;

7. For accommodation in Issyk-Kul, book a room at the “Talisman Village” Hotel according to the dates of arrival and departure. Details, contacts, prices (price lists of hotels) are specified on the Conference web-site;

8. Indicate the name of the Conference and the name of the Association (IAEEE) when booking a room in hotels, since discounts are provided to participants;

9. If you would like, you could visit directly the web-sites of hotels, which are listed on the price list;

10. Pay registration and publishing fees in accordance with the terms of payment.
