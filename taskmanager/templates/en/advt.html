Conference Announcement

Conference program is available on the Conference web-site and IAEEE web-site.

Information messages and invitations to participate in the upcoming conference will be send according to the conference organization schedule.

Prior to the conference, IAEEE Journal will be published, including materials from the III International Scientific and Practical Conference on Earthquake Engineering.

Speakers with research papers will be awarded diplomas.

Conference active participants without research papers can receive certificates of participation.

Attention! Conference will also be held online for participants who will not be able to take part in person (offline).

Conference Regulation

Working languages: Russian and English (accompanied by simultaneous translation).

PERFORMANCE TIME:

ꟷ welcome speeches and short messages – up to 5 minutes;

ꟷ invited papers – up to 30 minutes;

ꟷ plenary papers – up to 20 minutes;

ꟷ research papers in sections – up to 10 minutes.
