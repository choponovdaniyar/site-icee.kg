Dear colleagues, partners and friends!

The International Association of Experts on Earthquake Engineering (IAEEE) announces the preparation start for the III International Scientific and Practical Conference on Earthquake Engineering dedicated to the International Year of Sustainable Mountain Development (Conference website iсee.kg).

At the initiative of the President of the Kyrgyz Republic, in the Plenary meeting resolution of the UN General Assembly 76th session, 2022 was declared as the International Year of Sustainable Mountain Development.

This conference will be held in a hybrid form (online and offline) in Bishkek city and Issyk-Kul, Kyrgyzstan from July 4 to 9, 2022.

Please plan your participation, arrival and meeting in Bishkek city and Issyk-Kul from today!

Sincerely,

IAEEE President,

Ulugbek Begaliev
